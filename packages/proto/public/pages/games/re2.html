Resident Evil 2 January 25, 2009 Survival Horror 9/10 Capcom PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch The game begins with a truck driver hauling oil driving through the plains of Arklay County, entirely unaware of the recent quarantine of Raccoon City. Tired, eating a burger and listening to a talk radio program featuring an eye-witness to Zombies, the truck plows straight into a what seems like a woman walking on the highway. Panicked and scared, the driver gets out of the truck to inspect on the woman. But the woman rises up while his back is turned and attacks him. Elsewhere in the plains, police rookie Leon S. Kennedy and university student Claire Redfield stop off at the Mizoil Gas Station to fill up on petrol. The first to arrive investigates a smashing sound inside, and finds an Arklays County sheriff deputy being attacked by a Zombie. Zombies flock from the surrounding area to the gas station, cornering Leon and Claire. The two meet at the entrance, and evade the horde of zombies as they rush to the deputy's car, which Leon drives into the city. Claire Redfield Ada Wong
